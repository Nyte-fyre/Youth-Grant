2026–2027 Lee Lawrence Youth Grant Program Guidelines

The League of Women Voters of Diablo Valley is a non-partisan organization whose mission is to educate, inform and encourage civic engagement and to increase understanding of major public policy issues at all levels of government.

The Diablo Valley League established the Lee Lawrence Youth Grant Program from her bequest as a commemoration of Ms. Lawrence's life-long dedication to “making democracy work” and to youth engagement.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must be between the ages of 18 and 24 and able to demonstrate the capacity to complete the project being proposed.
  • The applicant must include with the application two letters of reference, which may be from teachers, mentors, advisors, or employers, not exceeding one page each.

Selection Criteria

Grants will be awarded to applicants whose proposal best meets the following criteria:

  • Meets the Eligibility Criteria.
  • Proposes a project that directly supports the work of the League of Women Voters Diablo Valley to educate, inform and encourage civic engagement aimed at “making democracy work,” and to increase understanding of major public policy issues at all levels of government.
  • Provides a proposal that shows how the project will be organized and completed within a reasonable timeframe and shows how the grant funding will support this plan.
  • Proposal includes a commitment to provide a final report to the League on the outcome of the funded project within 30 days and include a basic accounting with appropriate receipts of how the funds were expended.

Applications will be evaluated by the Diablo Valley Youth Grant Program Committee and approved by the LWVDV Board of Directors.
